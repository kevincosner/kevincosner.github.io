Synthetic Data

Interactive results comparisons on the following methods:

Single-pass joint-bilateral filtering (JBF), Kopf et al., ACM Trans. Graph., 26(3), 2007
Multi-resolution joint-bilateral upsampling (M-JBU), Richardt et al., Computer Graphics Forum (Proceedings of Eurographics), 31(2), 2012
The fast bilateral solver (FBS), Barron and Poole, Proceedings of European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV), 2016
Single-pass shared representative filtering (SRF), our method
Multi-resolution shared representative filtering (M-SRF), our method

All depth images are HD resolution (1280 x 720).
We allocated 15.0 ms for single-pass method (JBF and SRF); 7.5 milliseconds for multi-resolution methods (M-JBU and M-SRF).
We did not allocate time budget for FBS because it is difficult to control the execution time. It runs about 0.3 ~ 0.5 seconds. in the same environment.
